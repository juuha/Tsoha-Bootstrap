Waking Up

You wake up in a weird room with 4 doors, 1 on each of the walls surrounding you. You see a compass stuck on the floor in the middle of the room. It points north towards the door in front of you. You feel a sense of urgency, but aren't sure why.

What are you going to do?

  1. Go through the door left of you.
  2. Go through the door in front of you.
  3. Go through the door right of you.
